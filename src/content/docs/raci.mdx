<Muted>RACI is a project management framework defining roles for tasks: Responsible (does the work), Accountable (owns the outcome, only one per task), Consulted (provides input, two-way communication), and Informed (kept updated, one-way communication). A RACI chart is a simple matrix that clarifies who is responsible, accountable, consulted, and informed.</Muted>
